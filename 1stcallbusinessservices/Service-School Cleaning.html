We are 1st Call Cleaning Agency, your trusted partner in providing emergency cover cleaners and caretakers for schools. We understand the importance of maintaining a clean and safe learning environment, and our dedicated team is ready to step in whenever you need extra support.

Our Services for Schools:

  • Emergency Cover Cleaners: Unexpected situations can arise, leading to staff shortages or urgent cleaning needs. Our agency specialises in providing reliable and skilled emergency cover cleaners who can quickly fill in for your regular cleaning staff. Whether it's due to illness, vacation, or any other reason, we ensure that your school's cleanliness and hygiene standards are never compromised.
  • Caretaker Services: Schools require the expertise of caretakers to oversee the general maintenance and security of the premises. Our caretakers are experienced professionals who can handle a wide range of tasks, including maintenance, repairs, groundskeeping, and ensuring the safety of the school grounds.

Why Choose 1st Call Cleaning Agency:

  • Rapid Response: We understand the urgency of emergency situations, and our agency is equipped to provide swift and efficient solutions to your staffing needs.
  • Experienced Professionals: Our emergency cover cleaners and caretakers are thoroughly vetted, trained, and experienced in school environments, ensuring a seamless integration into your school's operations.
  • Flexibility: Our services are available around the clock, allowing you to reach out to us whenever you need assistance.
  • 24/7 Availability: We create customised cleaning plans to address your specific needs and budget. You can trust us to tailor our services to suit your requirements perfectly.
  • Quality Assurance: We take pride in delivering high-quality services. Our emergency cover cleaners and caretakers are dedicated to upholding the standards you expect for your school.
  • Cost-Effective Solutions: We offer competitive pricing for our services, making it cost-effective to partner with us for your staffing needs.
