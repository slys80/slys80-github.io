    Why Choose Us SLY

    Mission Statement

    To provide a quality and affordable education in an effective learning environment with extra moral activities to make our ward stand out.

    Vision statement

    Preparing our ward to stand out in all sphere of life and endeavours by providing them a qualitative education with a unique and well- developed curriculum sufficient to meet present educational demand.

    Toddlers

    Our After School Programme is set up for children under the ages of 2years – 3years and it essence is to enable our children with that age bracket to be very open to learning and stand out

    Our Infant After School Programme is set up for kids within the ages of 3years – 6years and it design to teach the to relate well and smart, keep them healthy and prone to learning, more important,help find their inner ability(ies) with the series of activities we have lined up.
